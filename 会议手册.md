Improvement in Smart Manufacturing Systems**

**摘要:** In a smart manufacturing system, a large number of sensors are
installed to monitor machine status, process variables, product quality,
and the overall system performance. It is always a challenging problem
on how to analyze those massive amounts of data effectively for cost
reduction and quality improvements in all manufacturing companies. This
presentation will discuss research opportunities, challenges, and
advancements in this important research area, especially how machine
learning concepts and algorithms can be used to solve challenging
quality improvement problems. Examples of ongoing research projects will
be used to articulate the frontiers of this research area. All examples
come from real data and problem in industrial production systems. This
presentation will emphasize the motivations of these research
undertakings: challenges to be overcome, new methods that were
developed, validation/implementation undertook, as well as the potential
impacts.

**报告题目：寿命试验数据的统计监测及其两种特殊的警报现象**

**摘要**：The advancement of production technology has led to continuous
improvement of product quality, with many modern products being
high-reliability and long-life. Life tests for highly reliable products
often take a long time even using accelerated life testing with
censoring. This poses significant challenges for manufacturers in timely
and accurately evaluating the product life characteristics and in
determining whether the production process is stable and controlled. In
this talk, I will introduce the quality control challenges inherent in
the production process under type-I censored life testing. In addition
to the dual impact of censoring time on the monitoring of censored
lifetime data, I will highlight two interesting signaling phenomena:
competing alarms and predetermined alarms. I will analyze the
probabilities of occurrence of these two alarms and their respective
impacts on the out-of-control performance, all while maintaining the
probability of false alarm at an acceptable level. The findings, derived
from Monte Carlo simulations, along with the related discussions, are
provided.

**报告人简介**：李乘龙，西北工业大学管理学院副教授、博士生导师，西安交通大学与香港城市大学联合培养博士。主要从事工业生产/服务运营过程质量控制与管理优化问题研究，在*IISE

**报告题目：Advances in System Reliability Assessment**

**摘要：**In this talk, we introduce several methods that address system
reliability assessment (SRA) challenges arising from limited lifetime
data due to high testing costs. Two complementary approaches are
developed: first, an exact method generalized from the Buehler
confidence limit that provides systematic statistic order selection
based on component reliability estimates and includes an algorithm for
constrained optimization problems; second, a high-order approximation
method called reliability-based Winterbottom-extended Cornish-Fisher
(R-WCF) expansion that uses polynomial adjustment techniques to
construct confidence limits for log-location-scale component lifetime
models. Numerical studies demonstrate that the exact method performs
well for small sample problems, while the R-WCF approach significantly
outperforms the delta method for highly reliable systems with
ultra-small sample sizes, providing practitioners with robust tools for
system reliability assessment under data-limited conditions.

**报告人简介：**李赵辉博士于2014年在中国科学技术大学获得理学学士学位，2020年获得中国科学院数学与系统科学研究院和香港城市大学联合培养博士学位。后在香港城市大学和佐治亚理工学院从事博士后研究工作。2023年7月加入中国科学院数学与系统科学研究院担任助理研究员。他的主要研究领域为系统可靠性，计算机实验与不确定性量化。

**报告题目：Maintenance and production control under imperfect online
monitoring with multi-sensor data**

**摘要:** In the manufacturing environment, sensors connected via the
Internet of Things are widely used to monitor the operational states of
systems, providing decision-makers with more refined support for
maintenance and production policy, thereby effectively reducing the cost
of system operation. However, the implementation of optimal operations
and the formulation of long-term strategies largely depend on the
quality of sensor data. This study proposes a framework that utilizes
multi-sensor monitoring to opti- mize maintenance and production
policies for a partially observable deteriorating system. These sensors
include both internal sensors within the system and external sensors
that can provide additional informa- tion, although obtaining
information from external sensors requires extra costs. Decision actions
include: continuing to operate the system using only internal sensor
information, paying a fee to obtain information from the external
sensor, or performing preventive maintenance. We analyze the structural
properties of the optimal policy and demonstrate that under specific
cost structures, the optimal policy can be divided into four regions
through three determined thresholds. Numerical examples verify the model
results and discuss the maximum willingness of decision-makers to pay
for external sensors with different levels of information.

**报告人简介：**赵秀杰，天津大学管理与经济学部工业工程系副教授。分别于2013年、2015年和2018年获得清华大学、宾夕法尼亚州立大学、香港城市大学工业工程专业学士、硕士与博士，"北洋学者英才计划"入选者。主要研究领域为可靠性与质量管理、实验设计、质保策略优化、系统维修优化与工业统计。主持1项自然科学基金面上项目及1项自然科学基金青年项目。

**报告题目：面向系统质量优化设计的复杂输入输出代理模型建模**

**摘要**：
传统复杂系统优化设计方法通常依赖成本高昂的物理实验或耗时的计算机仿真，而贝叶斯优化作为基于代理模型的小样本自适应主动搜索方法，能够在有限计算资源预算下高效搜索最优设计方案，已逐步成为复杂系统优化设计的首选方法。贝叶斯优化的性能高度依赖代理模型对系统响应的精确建模与不确定性量化。代理模型的不确定性量化有助于深入理解输入参数对系统输出的影响，提供可信的模型预测置信水平，为贝叶斯优化主动搜索策略提供决策依据。基于代理模型的贝叶斯优化已被广泛运用于先进制造系统的质量提升与高端装备的研发设计。本报告将探究复杂输入输出代理模型建模与不确定性量化，提升贝叶斯优化在复杂系统质量设计中的应用潜力。

**报告题目：Maximin distance designs with mixed continuous, ordinal, and
binary variables**

**摘要：**Computer experiments are pivotal for modeling complex
real-world systems. Maximizing information extraction and ensuring
accurate surrogate modeling necessitates space-filling designs, where
design points extensively cover the input domain. While substantial
research has been conducted on maximin distance designs for continuous
variables, which aim to maximize the minimum distance between points,
methods accommodating mixed-variable types remain underdeveloped. This
paper introduces the first general methodology for constructing maximin
distance designs integrating continuous, ordinal, and binary variables.
This approach allows flexibility in the number of runs, the mix of
variable types, and the granularity of levels for ordinal variables. We
propose three advanced algorithms, each rigorously supported by
theoretical frameworks, that are computationally efficient and scalable.
Our numerical evaluations demonstrate that our methods significantly
outperform existing techniques in achieving greater separation distances
across design points.

**报告人简介：**兰慧博士毕业于中国科学院数学与系统科学院系统所统计室，获得统计学博士学位，并获得香港城市大学联合培养计算机博士学位。2021年7月加入北京工业大学，目前是统计与数据科学系的讲师，一直从事计算机实验设计、机器学习和眼动数据分析方面的研究，已发表论文7篇。
